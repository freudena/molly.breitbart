The New York Times raves, "It's honestly a miracle she's survived this long."

Welcome to the official website of creative nonfiction author Molly Breitbart. Currently residing in New York City, Molly is a senior at Barnard College, Columbia University. Specialties include personal essay, oral history, ethnography and family history, and think pieces, especially if they work to promote the real experiences of women.

Despite descending from a people whose collective neuroses have been running amok since they fled through Egyptian deserts, Molly tries not to take herself too seriously. She is directionally challenged, a strong breakfast-for-dinner advocate, and plans to adopt a sizeable herd of corgis as soon as humanly possible.
